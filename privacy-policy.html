FlowBridge policy

Privacy Policy

FlowBridge is a desktop and browser productivity tool that helps users connect AI chat assistants with their local terminal workflow.

We collect only the information needed to operate the product, such as waitlist email addresses, support messages, license or subscription status, and basic website analytics.

The browser extension may read supported AI chat pages to detect code blocks and show FlowBridge controls. FlowBridge does not sell personal data, does not collect passwords, and does not collect payment card details.

Commands, terminal output, selected files, and AI chat content are used only when the user chooses to send or run them through FlowBridge.

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers such as Paddle. We do not store full payment card information on our servers.

For privacy questions, contact support@useflowbridge.com.
