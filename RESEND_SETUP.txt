FlowBridge Resend setup

Add these environment variables in Vercel:

RESEND_API_KEY
Your Resend API key.

SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY
Your Supabase service role key. Keep it secret.

BETA_DOWNLOAD_URL
https://github.com/medelhady/FlowBridge/releases/download/beta-v1/FlowBridge-Beta-v1.1-native-launcher.zip

TRACKED_DOWNLOAD_URL
https://useflowbridge.com/download

INSTALL_GUIDE_URL
https://useflowbridge.com/install

FROM_EMAIL
FlowBridge <support@useflowbridge.com>

PADDLE_WEBHOOK_SECRET
Copy this from Paddle after creating the webhook endpoint.

Paddle webhook endpoint:
https://useflowbridge.com/api/paddle-webhook

Recommended Paddle events:
transaction.completed
transaction.paid
subscription.created
subscription.activated

Important:
Use a verified domain in Resend before using support@useflowbridge.com.
For quick testing, Resend may allow onboarding@resend.dev depending on your account limits.
