FlowBridge Resend setup

Add these environment variables in Vercel:

RESEND_API_KEY
Your Resend API key.

SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY
Your Supabase service role key. Keep it secret.

FLOWBRIDGE_BETA_DOWNLOAD_URL
https://github.com/medelhady/FlowBridge/releases/download/beta-v1/FlowBridge-Beta-v1.zip

FLOWBRIDGE_FROM_EMAIL
FlowBridge <hello@flowbridge.app>

Important:
Use a verified domain in Resend before using hello@flowbridge.app.
For quick testing, Resend may allow onboarding@resend.dev depending on your account limits.
